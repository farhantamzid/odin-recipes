Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that layers wide, flat pasta with a rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and gooey cheese. To make a traditional lasagna, start by preparing a hearty meat sauce with ground beef, onions, garlic, and tomatoes, seasoned with Italian herbs like basil and oregano. Simmer the sauce until it’s thick and flavorful. Meanwhile, cook the lasagna noodles until al dente and prepare the béchamel sauce by melting butter, whisking in flour to create a roux, and slowly adding milk until it thickens into a smooth, creamy sauce.

To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish, followed by a layer of noodles. Next, add another layer of meat sauce, a generous sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, and a drizzle of béchamel. Repeat these layers until all the ingredients are used, finishing with a final layer of cheese on top. Bake the lasagna in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 45 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly. Let it rest for a few minutes before slicing to allow the layers to set, ensuring every bite is a perfect blend of flavors and textures.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3-4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat Oven: Set your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook Noodles: Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare Sauce: In a large skillet, cook ground beef (or sausage) with chopped onions and garlic until browned. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, diced tomatoes, and seasonings (salt, pepper, basil, oregano). Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Mix Cheese Filling: In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, an egg, and chopped fresh parsley.
  5. Assemble Lasagna: • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. • Add a layer of noodles. • Spread a layer of ricotta cheese mixture. • Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese. • Repeat layers (sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan) until you reach the top of the dish. Finish with a layer of meat sauce and a final sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  6. Bake: Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, until the top is golden and bubbly.
  7. Rest and Serve: Let the lasagna cool for about 15 minutes before serving.
