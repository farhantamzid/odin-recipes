Apple Spinach Salad

Description:

The Apple Spinach Salad is a refreshing and nutritious dish that combines the crispness of fresh spinach leaves with the sweet and tart flavors of apples. To prepare this salad, start by washing and drying a bunch of fresh spinach leaves, then thinly slice a couple of applesâ€”Granny Smith or Honeycrisp work well for their contrasting flavors. Toss the spinach and apple slices together in a large bowl, adding a handful of toasted pecans or walnuts for a delightful crunch.

To elevate the flavors, prepare a simple vinaigrette by whisking together olive oil, apple cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, honey, salt, and pepper. Drizzle the vinaigrette over the salad just before serving, ensuring everything is well-coated. For added texture and taste, sprinkle in some crumbled goat cheese or feta and dried cranberries. This Apple Spinach Salad is perfect as a light lunch or a vibrant side dish for any meal.

Ingredients:

  • Fresh spinach leaves
  • 2 apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp)
  • Handful of toasted pecans or walnuts
  • Olive oil
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Dijon mustard
  • Honey
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Crumbled goat cheese or feta (optional)
  • Dried cranberries (optional)

Steps:

  1. Wash and dry the spinach leaves.
  2. Thinly slice the apples.
  3. Toss the spinach and apple slices in a large bowl.
  4. Add toasted pecans or walnuts.
  5. Whisk together olive oil, apple cider vinegar, Dijon mustard, honey, salt, and pepper to make the vinaigrette.
  6. Drizzle the vinaigrette over the salad and toss to coat.
  7. (Optional) Sprinkle with crumbled goat cheese or feta and dried cranberries before serving.
